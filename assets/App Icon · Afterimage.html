How it reads at each size

At 100–200 px the chromatic offset reads beautifully — the violet trail behind the amber A is clearly legible, the rose mid-layer gives it warmth. This is the icon's home turf.

At 60–87 px the trails get tighter but the warm halo around the A still carries the brand feel.

At 16–32 px the chromatic effect compresses to a slight violet/pink halo around the amber A. Still distinctly Afterglow but loses the trail metaphor. If you want crisper favicons, I can add a simplified solid-amber A fallback that kicks in below 40 px — say the word.
